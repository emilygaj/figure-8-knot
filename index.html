CLICK ON THE KNOT TO LEARN HOW TO TIE IT.

Start with your rope, and create a "bight" (or a bend) in the rope.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Wrap the tail of the rope around the other side of the bight.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Cross the tail fully behind, so that you have a loop.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Put the tail through the top of the loop. Now you have the first half of a figure-8 follow through!

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Pull the tail of your figure-8 knot through a carabiner to make another bight.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Bring the tail behind the top strand of rope & through the top loop.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Cross the tail completely behind the bottom of your knot.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Pull the tail between the top loop and the two strands of rope infront of it.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Cross the tail in front your knot, then follow the strand from the top loop behind the two strands & through the bottom loop.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Pull the very top strand in front of the one under it.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

Pull each of the four loose strands separately to tighten your knot.

HOVER ON THE KNOT TO SEE THE STEP COMPLETED.

CONTINUE

You have successfully tied the figure-8 follow through knot!

CLICK ON YOUR KNOT TO RESTART.
